If you're the proud owner of a Blackstone griddle, then you're probably looking for some recipes to try out on it. Well, you've come to the right place!

We've gathered nine of the best recipes that will show off your griddle's amazing cooking capabilities. So get ready to fire up your griddle and start cooking!

Outdoor Griddle owners rejoice! If you own a Blackstone griddle, you probably cant wait to start cooking. BUT, there's a problem....

There are too many options running through your head and frankly, you aren't sure where to start. I get it, I really do.

So, I have put together a list of 9 of my favorite Blackstone Griddle recipes to share with you to get you started.

But, dont get me wrong, you dont need a Blackstone Griddle, these recipes work for ALL griddles!

From the easy breakfast ideas, to unique desserts you didn't even know your griddle could make!

Jalapeno Popper Grilled Cheese on the Griddle

Anyone who loves grilled cheese and jalapeno poppers knows that the two are a match made in heaven.

Let's be honest, what could be better than enjoying this tasty combo outdoors on a warm day?

Blackstone griddles are perfect for making grilled cheese, and there's no need to even turn on the oven. Just fire up the grill and you're ready to go.

Best of all, this recipe is easy to make and only requires a few simple ingredients.

So if you're looking for a fun way to enjoy your next outdoor meal, give this jalapeno popper grilled cheese recipe a try!

Blackstone Griddle Stromboli on a Griddle

Stromboli is a tasty, fun, and easy to make outdoor griddle dish. I know what your'e thinking..."Stromboli, on a griddle?" Yep! and it tastes great!

Check out this recipe from OrWhateverYouDo to get started on this awesome dish!

Blackstone Grill Chicken Wings

Who doesn't love chicken wings? Whether its game day or a night after a long day at work, chicken wings always hit the spot.

This easy to follow recipe is a great one to start trying some unique dish options on your Blackstone aside from the standard "eggs and bacon".

Biscuits on the Griddle

Making breakfast biscuits on a Blackstone griddle is a fun and easy way to make a delicious breakfast.

The outdoor griddle makes it easy to cook breakfast for a crowd, and the biscuits taste awesome.

Blackstone griddles are easy to use, and they make it even easier to cook breakfast for a crowd.

Tortilla Pizza on the Griddle

If you're looking for a quick and easy pizza recipe that tastes awesome, look no further than tortilla pizza on a Blackstone griddle.

This dish is perfect for an outdoor griddle like the Blackstone grill, and it only takes a few minutes to make. Plus, it's a great way to use up any leftover tortillas you might have around.

You'll want to eat this pizza again and again!

Breakfast Quesadilla's on a Griddle

Who doesn't love breakfast food? More importantly, who doesn't love a great breakfast on a griddle?

Ever wanted to make your own fried rice? Well now you can with this easy recipe. Now you take a fan favorite and tweak it to make it your own with this awesome fried rice recipe.

It's so good and tastes just like the kind they serve at that hibachi restaurant, but without all those pesky carbs!

Nachos on the Griddle

Everyone loves nachos and if not, they should! This take on nachos that can be made on your Blackstone grill are pretty awesome.

From the cheese to the toppings, you can make these your own!
