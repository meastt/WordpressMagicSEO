Pellet grills with WIFI have become increasingly common. Not only are they fun and easy to use, but can also be controlled directly through a WIFI connection. 

These grills are quite different from a typical grill and rely upon sophisticated technology that uses WIFI to connect to a phone. 

The last few years have seen numerous grill manufacturers integrate WIFI functionality into their products.

Through this functionality, you can control the pellet grill even when you are away from home. 

A common question that potential grill buyers have is, ‘is WIFI worth it on pellet grills’?

Well, it allows you to monitor the grill temperature, change the cooking temperature, and keep track of the wood pellets present in the grill hopper – remotely.

Although you can use wireless thermometers for remote temperature monitoring, changing the grill temperature without being physically present is only possible through a WIFI-enabled pellet grill. 

However, WIFI is not a prerequisite for good food, and non-WIFI pellet grills will cook food just fine. 

How Does WIFI Work On a Pellet Grill?

A pellet grill has always been popular among meat lovers.

However, with the inclusion of WIFI, manufacturers have taken a major step in making cooking easier and more convenient, ensuring that the user puts in as little effort as possible during the cooking process. 

To activate the WIFI functionality on a pellet grill, you will have to download the dedicated application and connect it to the grill’s WIFI.

Once the connectivity has been established, you will be able to control the grill directly through your phone. Your phone can be used to:

  • Turn the grill on and off
  • Control and monitor the cooking temperatures
  • Set alarms and timers when the temperature needs to be changed
  • Program your grill so that it starts cooking at pre-determined times

WIFI pellet grills have a dedicated app that allows you to connect the grill to your home’s WIFI and, consequently, control it remotely. 

Benefits of WIFI Pellet Grills:

Without diving deep into the benefits of WIFI pellet grills, it can be hard to determine if these grills are worth it or not.

Below are some of the main benefits of a pellet grill with WIFI:

Alerts and Timers:

These contemporary grills often come with digital timers that you can use to turn the grill off after some time.

This is ideal for people who want to maximize their time and do not want to spend long periods monitoring the pellet grill. 

Another related benefit is that of alerts, which notify you once the food is fully cooked. With these notifications, you will not have to worry about overcooking, ever again. 

Temperature Control:

A major benefit of WIFI pellet grills is that they allow you to remotely control and monitor the internal grill temperature.

This can be done either by assessing the meat probes or by controlling the augers.

As long as you have an internet connection, you can perform this monitoring and control from pretty much anywhere on the planet. 

Greater Cooking Precision:

Traditional pellet grills may have forced you to make a lot of educated and not-so-educated guesses throughout the cooking process.

However, with the advent of WIFI pellet grills, you can get rid of this guessing game once and for all.

These high-tech grills are capable of wirelessly transmitting the internal grill temperature information to your phone or any other smart device, allowing you unprecedented control over the grilling process. 

You can obtain real-time feedback, and use these insights to make timely changes.

This way, you will know if the meat is being overcooked or undercooked, and make amends to ensure the perfect result. 

Customized Cook Cycles:

A WIFI pellet grill is certainly a revolutionary innovation, allowing cooks to combine grilling techniques with different recipes.

In other words, you can look for pre-programmed cooking cycles that have been tailor-made for specific kinds of meat, seafood, and vegetables.

The cooking cycles are then matched with your preferred temperatures and cooking times, allowing you to come up with a dish that is perfect in every way. 

Hands-Free Cooking: 

People often plan BBQ events because they want to spend more time with their families and friends.

With a WIFI pellet grill, you can let the WIFI handle the bulk of the work, while you catch up with your loved ones and give them the kind of time and attention that they are worthy of. 

Similarly, while your pellet grill WIFI takes care of the cooking, you can take care of other tasks and chores.

Through the dedicated smartphone application, you can rest assured that, even in your physical absence, the grilling will not be compromised in any way whatsoever. 

Final Assessment – is WIFI Worth It On Pellet Grills?

YES, we certainly feel that WIFI pellet grills are worth the money.

Not only do they provide a remote cooking experience, but they also ensure that you are kept in the loop even when you are physically away from your grill. 

Moreover, these grills contain accurate and sensitive sensors that can inform you when the meat has reached or exceeded a particular temperature.

As a result, you will not have to stick around your grill (or keep returning frequently) to make sure that everything is in order. 

WIFI connectivity has become such an integral part of our lives, allowing us a great deal of control over our homes and devices even when we are away.

A WIFI-enabled pellet grill not only saves energy and time but can also maintain consistency in temperature throughout the grilling process. 

As we mentioned, WIFI functionality is not essential for the actual cooking part.

However, this functionality can certainly add a great deal of flexibility, convenience, and consistency to the whole process.

If that is what you are looking for, you should certainly consider opting for a pellet grill with WIFI.
