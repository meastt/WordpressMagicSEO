The answer to this question is that some Weber grill models can be built into structures, while others cannot.

Currently, the company offers two models that can be built-in – the Summit S-660 and S-460.

Both these models are available in both natural gas and propane versions and have been designed exclusively for in-built structures. 

If you decide to build into a Weber model other than the S-660 or S-440, the company voids the warranty on its grills.

This is because the other models have not been optimized for built-in use.

Therefore, the company cannot be sure if they are safe to be built into and how they will behave with in-built structures. 

Why Can We Not Build Into Other Weber Grill Models?

A build-in grill model is developed in a different manner than regular grill models.

Build-in models require unique testing and development, and other ventilation concerns also have to be adequately addressed. 


How Long Does Assembling a Weber Grill Take?

Using just manual tools, assembling a Weber grill should take you somewhere between an hour to 90 minutes.

If you utilize a power screwdriver, you can get the job done considerably faster (somewhere around 40 to 50 minutes).

Once you have completed the assembly, you will require another 10 to 20 minutes to clean up all the recyclable cardboard. 

Is it hard to assemble a Grill?

The truth is, you can totally assemble a grill all by yourself.

However, the difficulty level will vary on the grill model that you opt for.

Some models come with around a dozen parts, while others arrive with hundreds of components that need to be put together. 

What is the Cost of Installing an In-Built BBQ?

If you decide to go for a built-in grill, you can expect the cost of the grill to be anywhere between $800 and $3,500.

Alternatively, you can opt for a drop-in grill – the average for a drop-in grill will be similar to that of a built-in one, but the price range will be between $800 and #$2,500. 

What is the Difference between a Weber Spirit and a Weber Genesis?

The key differences between a Weber Spirit and a Weber genesis are in terms of heat output capability and additional/customized features.

However, when you compare the two grills in terms of primary cooking aspects, the Weber Spirit series, across the board, is less expensive than the Weber Genesis series. 

Is a Weber Grill Duel Fuel?

Every Weber grill sold in the USA can be used either with natural gas or with propane, which makes the grills duel fuel. 

What Are the Benefits of a Built-in Grill?

Compared to stand-alone grills, built-in grills tend to be bigger. Other than that, a built-in grill often includes countertops that can be used during food preparation.

In addition, you will be able to store your chopping boards, utensils, and anything else that you need, right next to your grill.

This way, you will not have to remain inside to prepare the food before being able to go out to the grill. 

Where Are Weber Grills Made?

Every Weber grill is built in the USA.

The first Weber grill was made in the year 1952, when George Stephen used the Weber Brothers Metal Works located in Mount Prospect II, to build his kettle grill.  


Can the Weber iGrill 3 be used on any Grill?

Ideally, the iGrill 3 should not be used with any grill; the iGrill 3 has been designed specifically for the Weber Spirit II and Weber Genesis II.

However, if you do want to use it with a different grill, you can give it a shot.

However, the iGrill 3 will not fit as well or be as comfortable as it would be with the grill models that it has been specifically designed for. 

How is the Weber iGrill 3 Different from the Weber iGrill 2?

The key differences between the two models are in terms of hook-up and compatibility with the grills, as well as the battery time.

Not only does the iGrill 3 attach better to the grills, it also has a 250-hour battery life (compared to 200 hours for the iGrill 2). 

Do Weber Grills go on Sale?

Every couple of years, specific Weber grill models will be up for sale during the spring season.

This is usually the time when the brand is introducing new models, and stores are looking to sell off the old versions to make way for the new ones.

You can find significant discounts and price cuts on certain Weber models.

However, finding these discounted rates will require you to physically visit the stores, since these prices are not marketed online. 

What type of Warranty does Weber Grills Have?

Weber is one of the most popular grill brands going around, and one of the main reasons behind this popularity is its powerful warranty.

Every Weber grill comes with a ten-year warranty, which adds to the customers’ peace of mind and confidence.

Every grill is integrated with the G4S Weber grilling system – a system that has been the result of many years of trials and learning. 

Are Built-in Grills Worth It?

If you are looking for a simple, solidly-built grill, there is absolutely nothing wrong with that.

However, if you are a grill fanatic and cooking nerd looking for all the whistles and bells, a built-in grill is certainly worth the extra money and effort.
