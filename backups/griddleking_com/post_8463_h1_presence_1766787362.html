Cooking On The Patio Oven

One great plus to having this specific pizza oven attachment is the speed at which the device cooks your pizza.

This baby will have your pies fully cooked in as little as two and a half minutes!

This is possible thanks to the pizza cooking technology patented that uses two large stones in the heating process.

Both of these stones are made of cordierite with one being in the shape of a rectangle and the other taking on a more rounded shape.

This cooking technology combined with the surface area of the grill that the oven is attached to will allow you to cook some of the most tender and crispy pizzas from up to fifteen inches in diameter.

This Outdoor Pizza Oven Gets Hot!

The Blackstone Steel Outdoor Pizza Oven Add On has some amazing and impressive heating capabilities.

Just like most other ovens of greater size, this little attachment has the capacity to heat up a pizza that measures up to five quarters of a foot in diameter to crisp and tender perfection. Do not be fooled by its smaller size.

Of course, it will still need the grill and propane. With these two things, the interior of the oven can reach up to 700 Degrees Fahrenheit, which is more than enough for any pizza!
